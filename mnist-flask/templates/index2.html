Found a feather?


The Aviary is designed to help you identify what species of bird a feather may have come from. We leverage cutting-edge computer vision approaches to identify key features on user-uploaded pictures of feathers, identify potential matches, and deliver a customized report containing species information, our confidence rating on the match, and links to more information.


Your Turn


Ready to try it for yourself? Use the file upload tool below to upload your image or to use a default image. For best results we recommend taking a picture of the feather in a well-lit area in front of a solid colored background. The more clearly the feather stands out from the background, the better results you'll get.
